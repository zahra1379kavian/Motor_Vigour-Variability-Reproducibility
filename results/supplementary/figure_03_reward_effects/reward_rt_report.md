# Reward Effects on Inverse Reaction Time

- Low reward codes: [0.0, 1.0]
- High reward codes: [5.0]
- Excluded reward codes: [-5.0]
- Excluded subject/session entries: PSPD017 session 1 (ON)
- Included finite 1/RT trials: 5393
- Subjects: 18

## Primary Paired Result

Subject-level inverse RT high - low reward delta = -0.0072 1/s (95% CI -0.0120, -0.0025; paired t p = 0.00517; Wilcoxon p = 0.0056; n = 18).

Trial-level fixed-effect regression reward_high beta = -0.0079 1/s (95% CI -0.0123, -0.0036; p = 0.000373), adjusted for subject, medication, GVS, and run with subject-clustered standard errors.

Positive high-minus-low inverse RT values mean high-reward trials were faster.
